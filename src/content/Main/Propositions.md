# Propositions
declarative sentences that are either **True** or **False**. 
The area of logic that handles propositions is called **Propositional Logic** or **Propositional calculus**.

## Notation
- **True** : **1** or **T**
- **False** : **0** or **F**
- **propositions** : p,q,r,etc
